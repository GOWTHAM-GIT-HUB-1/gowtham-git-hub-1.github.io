Hospital Emergency Dashboard

This dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of hospital emergency room activity for a specific month. It tracks the total number of patients who visited the ER and the average time they waited to be seen. It also measures how satisfied patients were with their care and the number of patients who were referred elsewhere after their ER visit.The dashboard further categorizes patients by their admission status (whether they were admitted to the hospital or not) and shows the percentage of patients seen within a target timeframe.It also presents a breakdown of ER visits by patient age group and gender.The dashboard identifies the days of the week and the hours of the day with the highest patient volume.Finally, it illustrates the number of patients referred from different departments within the hospital and provides a breakdown of patients by race.

HR Analytics dashboard(EXCEL)

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce trends, focusing on employee attrition, job satisfaction, and departmental stability. It helps in identifying key factors influencing employee turnover and highlights areas that need attention for better retention strategies.

The dashboard analyzes attrition across different job roles and departments, showing which positions experience higher employee exits. It also evaluates job satisfaction levels, revealing potential reasons behind employee disengagement. Additionally, demographic insights, such as age groups and marital status, offer a deeper understanding of employee retention patterns. By identifying trends in workforce stability, the dashboard serves as a valuable tool for HR teams to improve employee engagement, address critical pain points, and develop data-driven strategies for reducing attrition and enhancing workplace satisfaction.

AmazonPrime Content Analysis

This project is a visual analysis of Amazon Prime Video’s content library, designed to give a clear picture of what kind of shows and movies are available on the platform. The dashboard breaks down the content by type—like movies vs. TV shows—and shows how content has grown over the years. It also explores viewer age ratings to understand who the content is aimed at, and highlights the most popular genres. There’s a world map that shows where the content comes from, giving a global perspective, and it also tracks how much new content has been added recently. Overall, the goal of this project is to make it easy to spot trends and patterns in Amazon Prime’s content strategy using a clean, interactive visual format.

Pizza Sales Performance

This dashboard gives a clear overview of pizza sales performance. It highlights which days and times people order the most, showing that weekends and certain hours in the afternoon are the busiest. It breaks down sales by different pizza categories and sizes, pointing out that one particular category and a specific size are the top contributors to total sales. There’s also insight into which pizzas are best-sellers and which ones don’t perform as well. A section shows which types of pizzas are bringing in the most revenue. Overall, this dashboard helps understand customer buying patterns, popular products, and what’s working best for the business in terms of sales and timing. It’s useful for making smart business decisions based on actual data.

Furniture Sales Dashboard

This dashboard shows how furniture sales are performing across different states in the U.S. It highlights the total sales, profit, and quantity sold, along with how they have changed compared to the previous year. You can clearly see which months had higher sales, with a peak in the last month of the year. It also breaks down the most common shipping methods and how long deliveries usually take. The map shows which states and cities brought in the most sales, making it easy to spot strong-performing regions. The product section reveals which furniture categories are generating the highest revenue, with one type standing out as the top seller. Overall, this dashboard gives a full view of sales trends, shipping behavior, and regional performance, helping businesses make informed decisions.

The dashboard is designed to offer quick insights for decision-makers and help identify areas of growth and improvement across different product categories and regions.
